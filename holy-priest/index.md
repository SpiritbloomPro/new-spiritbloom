Welcome to the holy priest guide! Holy priests are an effective healer that specialize in having multiple tools for every situation. We are one of world of warcrafts jack of all trade healers being able to specialize in effective group heals or excellent spot healing. This specialization has a low barrier to entry with some fun interactions to allow people who master it to shine.
